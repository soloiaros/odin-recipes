Oatmeal Banana Bread

Description:

Tired of your regular meals or just want a healthier alternative to a regular cake? If that's the case, get ready to cook!

Ingredients:

  • 75g Rolled oats
  • 75g Milk of preference
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • 80g Greek yogurt
  • 2 Chicken eggs (medium)
  • 10g Chocolate chips
  • 120g Banana
  • 5g Honey
  • 5g Raisins

Steps:

  1. Preheat you oven to 200 degrees Celsius;
  2. In a single bowl, put sliced banana, oats, milk, baking powder, 30g of Greek yogurt and 2 chicken eggs;
  3. Blend all these ingredients together with a mixer;
  4. Throw in chocolate chips and whick with a spoon;
  5. Pour the resulting mixture into a baking dish (mind that the bread may become much bigger in size while baking);
  6. Put the dish into the oven, set the timer to 20 minutes;
  7. When fully baked, take the dish from the oven and place the bread onto a plate;
  8. Decorate with the remaining Greek yogurt and raisins;
  9. Enjoy this meal with a cup of coffee or other drink of your preference!
