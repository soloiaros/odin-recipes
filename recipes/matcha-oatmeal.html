Green Matcha Oatmeal

Description:

Let's see, you love matcha but also want to have a savory and satiating, nutrient-rich breakfast? We've gor you!

Ingredients:

  • 60g Rolled oats
  • 2 tsp Green matcha tea (powdered)
  • 50ml Milk of choice
  • 50ml hot water
  • 5g honey
  • 10g blueberries (can be substituted by raisins)
  • 1 banana or other fruit of preference

Steps:

  1. Put rolled oats in a bowl (take an appropriately sized one);
  2. Put you milk on a heated stove and let it heat up until nearly boiling, then put matcha in and stir for 10-20 seconds. Take the resulting mixture off the stove;
  3. Pour in hot water followed by the milk-and-matcha mixture, place a towel on top of the bowl and let it sit for 5 minutes (depending on the type of oats);
  4. While waiting, slice up the banana;
  5. Put the towel off the bowl, decorate with blueberries and bananas;
  6. Enjoy you beautiful and healthy breakfast with a cup of coffe or on its own!
